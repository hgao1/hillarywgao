Hi,
I'm a former undergraduate of Physics and Art History
at NYU (class of 2020).
In the past, I've worked with Professor David Grier
in acoustic holography, Central Park with sculpture
conservation, and Professor Bud Mishra with COVID19
simulations.
I'm particularly passionate about merging physics and art
with a bend towards applications of science that help others.
In addition to that, I'm also xinterested in performance art,
pushing the questioning the boundary of
performers/audience members, and contemporary performance.
I currently work at a soft matter start-up (that works with optics),
but in my free time, you can find me
building robots (to watch feral cats), riding my bike around Brooklyn,
and falling asleep to movies.
