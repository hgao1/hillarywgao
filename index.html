Hillary?

Hi,
I am a creator/writer/(and sometimes unwilling) performer based in Ridgewood, Queens.
I did my undergrad in Physics & Art History @ NYU (2020).
While there, I worked w. David Grier in acoustic holography, Central Park in sculpture conservation, and Bud Mishra w. COVID19 simulations.
Recently, I have been writing (sort of) plays.
I am working on one about humans becoming animals at dinner ("would you set the table if I asked you to?").
And also tinkering around with another one about queer lambs being reborn and sacrificed ("a lamb with spot and blemish").
I am always down to collab-- please reach out @ hillary.gao1@gmail.com!
