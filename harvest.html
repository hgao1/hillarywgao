h a r v e s t i n g

to harvest...
an idea, relationships, artwork.
Harvesting is a seasonal performance party,
an informal event,
an uncuratable festival/event,
where we share in the witnessing/participation of
creative processes of artists.
There are no limitations: all disciplines and stages of work are fair game!
Everyone attending is invited to share their work in informal performances,
with a variety of art-forms:
performance, stand-up, music, dance, visual art, etc...
The media is partially archived on the website using the format of a 2D video-game format.
