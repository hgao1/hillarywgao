h a r v e s t i n g

to harvest...
an idea, relationships, artwork. What does it mean?
Each piece of art starts with a seed in the ground, an idea that begins to grow and often takes time to ripen.
The idea behind harvesting is to provide artists with a space to test the fruits of their labor,
whether it's already a piece that's been shown, or a piece that's on its way to being finished.

Harvesting is a seasonal performance party,
an uncuratable festival/event,
where we share in the witnessing/participation of
creative processes of artists in the pandemic.
Everyone attending is invited to share their work in informal performances,
with a variety of art-forms:
performance, stand-up, music, dance, visual art, etc...
The media is partially archived on the website using the format of a 2D video-game format.
